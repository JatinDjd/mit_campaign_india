Top Grocery Delivery App Development Features

With years of expertise in creating on-demand grocery delivery app solutions, we take care to deliver feature-rich grocery app solutions. You can enjoy a proper mix of essential and advanced features with the top Grocery delivery app development company.

  • Customer App
  • Driver App
  • Admin Panel

Customer App

Customer Application enables customers to browse multiple grocery stores and book grocery orders for doorstep deliveries.

  1. Social Media Login

    The users can connect their social accounts and share their experiences with friends, family, and the world.

  2. Search Nearby Stores

    The users can search for the nearby grocery stores on the app, compare prices, and order from the desired store.

  3. Add To Cart

    Users can add the products and items they like to the cart for purchase later.

  4. Add To Wishlist

    Users can avail coupons and promo codes to buy their favourite products at a discounted rate.

  5. Live Order Tracking

    The app enables customers to track the real-time location of the orders placed by them.

  6. inApp Payments

    Integrate In-app payment that enables customers to make online payments in whichever way they want.

  7. Ratings & Reviews

    The users can rate their experience with the grocery delivery services in this section.

  8. Transaction History

    Users can see all their transactions made through the app in this section.

Driver App

Driver application enables drivers to fulfill grocery orders and deliver them to customer destinations.

  1. Manage Availability

    Drivers can change their status on the app as available or not available using this feature.

  2. Order Requests

    The drivers get notifications of all the orders placed through customers on the app.

  3. Manage Payments

    The drivers can view, track, and manage all the payments made by customers on the app.

  4. Live Tracking

    Live Tracking feature enables drivers to track the delivery locations using GPS and track routes on the map.

  5. Profile

    Drivers have to make their profile on the app by entering personal details like name, contact with important details like license, certificates, etc.

  6. Ratings & Reviews

    Drivers can also rate their experiences with any customers and also report any misbehaving customers.

  7. Transaction History

    They can keep track of all the orders assigned and deliveries made.

  8. Push Notifications

    The drivers receive notifications of new orders placed and deliveries and payments made.

Admin Panel

Admin panel helps grocery business owners manage customers, grocery orders, inventory, and deliveries.

  1. Authentication

    The Admin can authenticate and manage to provide access to users and drivers.

  2. Manage Dashboard

    The Admin can manage all the activities using the Grocery delivery app dashboard.

  3. Orders Management

    The Admin can track all the orders placed, orders due, and orders delivered.

  4. Coupons, Discounts, and Offers

    The Admin can create new offers, provide discount coupons, and also change the offers.

  5. Manage Add / Edit / Delete

    Any restaurant, user, driver, product, or service can be added, edited, or deleted using the admin panel.

  6. Sub-admin creation

    The Admin can create sub-admins and assign them tasks for quick activity and process.

  7. Content Management

    The Admin can manage the content on the pages with the help of this feature.

  8. Reports

    The Admin can view reports generated at a regular interval to monitor performances.
